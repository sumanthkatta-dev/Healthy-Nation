BMI (Body Mass Index)

A body mass index (BMI) calculator is a tool that estimates body fat based on a person's weight and height. It can help you:

  • Screen for weight categories
  • A BMI calculator can help identify weight categories that may lead to health problems.
  • Assess your risk of disease
  • A higher BMI is associated with an increased risk of obesity-related diseases, such as heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and stroke.
  • Evaluate your overall health
  • A BMI calculator can help you monitor your weight in relation to other body factors.

You can use a BMI calculator to enter your weight and height in standard or metric units to calculate your BMI.
